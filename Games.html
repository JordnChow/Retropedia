Nintendo's Greatest Games

Donkey Kong

One of Nintendo's oldest game franchises, Donkey Kong saw its birth with Nintendo's arcade era, and quickly reemerged and adapted to console gaming. Owned by Nintendo itself, Donkey Kong features the player following the main character, a gorilla named Donkey Kong,and his friends. The games are usually platform runners, with quick thinking and fast reaction times keeping players coming back for more.

Splatoon

Splatoon is a fast paced third person shooter game, predominantly in 2 teams of 4 players shooting and fighting each other in a set map. Soaring to popularity after its release on the Wii U, but truly made a name for itself after the release of splatoon2 and 3 on the switch. A more recent game-franchise, splatoon is still highly played and receives constant updates to hook players.

Pokemon

Pokemon, meaning Pocket monster, is the world's biggest franchise. It’s video-game series was produced by game freak, and later shared with the pokemon company, and nintendo. Pokemon started featuring on Nintendo consoles since its first ever games, Red, Blue and Green. Originally only exclusive to japan, Pokemon saw a massive rise in popularity, and became a household name worldwide. New consoles came with new pokemon games, and a large driving factor for purchasing nintendo’s consoles and handhelds where to play the pokemon games.

Pokemon usually released about two-three versions of the same game for every console release, only having minor differences between each version. Over time, pokemon became a staple in gaming, and with a growing card and media branch, soar to international fame. To this day, pokemon is the largest grossing franchise of all time, and releases new games for nintendo consoles.

Mario

One of the most iconic franchises in history, mario came from humble beginnings as a simple platform running game. It follows Mario, and his brother Luigi, both Italian plumbers who jump over and kill monsters and obstacles coming from pipes. Nowadays, mario has seen many changes, from incredible graphics, new sub-franchises like the mario-kart series, as well as a new soon to come movie. Mario is highly acclaimed by nintendo fans, and continues to this day to be immensely popular on recent consoles like the switch and switch-lite

Zelda

This icon of a game series first released on Nintendo consoles in 1986. It follows the heroic journey of Link, a young elf who fights to protect kingdoms from adversaries. There are currently 29 zelda games out there. The most recent one, Breath of the Wild, was a huge hit, mainly due to the nostalgia drawing old players, as well as new and much improved graphics inviting new players to the game. Many players point out that pokemon and zelda have many similarities, being both adventure games with a basic structure, but a lot of player-choices.
